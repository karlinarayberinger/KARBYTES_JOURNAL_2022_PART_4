KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_105

It is about 4PM on a sunny Wednesday. I am sitting on a hill between Castro Valley and Hayward on my way to buy some THC pills from the local cannabis dispensary with the $40 in cash I withdrew while purchasing a bottle of water at Safeway. The temperature today is moderate. There is a slight breeze and no clouds in the blue sky. The new HP laptop I am using has a sturdier shell and more screen brightness than my previous laptop did.

I have not gone home since three days ago. I have been spending most of my time hanging out in urban and suburban areas and using public electrical outlets, public drinking fountains, public bathrooms, grocery stores, and coffee shops to get replenished as “needed”. (Of course I am “spoiling” myself like this might be the last summer I ever get this much free time to do whatever I want within the confines of the governing structures I live inside of. As much as I fancy being my own boss, I seem to have no choice but to be talked at, spied on, and micromanaged by law enforcement personnel. I do not mean to inconvenience anyone, but I suspect that I am pissing some people off for being such a public eyesore, for talking out loud to myself at times, and for spending all my time and resources making myself feel good instead of overtly serving others and making others the focus of my life at least 25% of the time).

Today I plan to work on a simple word counter application which will solely consist of a JavaScript file, a Cascading Style Sheet file, and a Hyper Text Markup Language file. I think I am done inserting drawings into my primary site. If I am especially diligent, I will get the entire primary site up to date by 04_JULY_2022. (I am afraid that, as soon as I get my website up to date, I am going to panic because my imaginary and not so imaginary audience will start bombarding me with depression-mongering “news” about how I have nothing left to prove and that it is now time for me to stop being a self indulgent camper and start being a much more humble looking, quiet, demure, people-oriented customer service agent wannabe who is seriously devoted to getting and keeping a local job so that I can earn my keep and quell some of that doom-mongering coming my way from those who criticize me 24 hours per day every day).

After I get some stuff from the weed store, I plan on going back to Dublin/Pleasanton for a few hours (if not all night) to work on the word counter app and to avoid the doom-mongering radio signals I seem to get while in the 510 area code regions. (The 925 (i.e. Dublin/Pleasanton/Livermore) region and the 415 (i.e. San Francisco/Daly City) region seems to be more accommodating of my chosen personality. The radio ambience in those regions seem more accommodating of tech workers and people with affluent tastes. No offense, the 510 region feels a bit “low brow”, slummy, and petulant. It feels like I have to always be “on guard” in the 510 places like people are ready to pick a fight with me and to tell me to shut up and look more downtrodden and that this world belongs to those who prefer to spend lots of time with young children. I am sick of being around little kids, quite frankly. I wish I had my own apartment for one which I did not have to pay rent for which I could go to and enjoy browsing the Internet and doing art and studying to my heart’s content without being told to “tone it down” and to not look so stressed out and/or enthusiastic because it might upset the little kids and their equally deprived adult caregivers. I am sick of having to hide my preference to be affluent, non familial, tech obsessed, and opinionated and engrossed in “esoteric” and “politically incorrect” topics on the Internet. I am impatient to get out of the places which make me feel stifled, put up with, needled, nagged, ridiculed, and drained of enthusiasm just so that the small minded breeders can feel like they rule the area while I cower at their feet and thank them profusely for allowing me to breathe at all).

I put up with a lot of inconvenience by being next to those I consider to be too “low brow” for me. I think they can hear my thoughts because, as soon as I think something taboo, I hear people in the background booing and barking. I do not get a real break from being in public. If I want more wiggle room to hear my own thoughts and to not be so frequently interrupted, barked at, and suppressed because of other people’s fragile and immature egos, I have to hike for hours to relatively secluded wilderness areas or take the BART train to places where it is okay to be a professional and not just a brood butler.

Well, I got to share my thoughts and feelings in writing to some extent. This journal entry is a bit trite and shallow compared to what I think it would be if I got more time away from “the people” who seem to drain my mental energy to the point that I turn into a dunce. I do not want to work as a customer service agent or in hospitality ever again. I think such jobs are good for absolute beginners; not for people who have qualifications and interests in more cerebral jobs. I do not think it is possible to be a socialite and an intellectual at the same time.
