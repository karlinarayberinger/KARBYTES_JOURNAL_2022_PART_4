KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_109

I spent a few hours hanging out at “The Bunny Bush” near the East Dublin BART station. Though I have a pack of ten THC pills (and each pill contains 25 milligrams of THC), I do not plan on using those pills for at least one more day because I have been on a sober streak and want to keep milking it for all it’s worth. I deal with chronic anxiety, depression, and anger due to the philosophical thoughts I have and due to the fact that the police have been harassing me almost nonstop daily by bombarding me with antagonistic and distracting auditory messages which I think they transmit to my cochlea using photon beams (or to implants in my skull using radio waves). I do not know how the police are able to decipher the content of my thoughts so instantaneously and accurately. Perhaps my biological activity is not that hard to decode noninvasisively and, hence, to me it looks like they are able to peer into my mind and reproduce the qualia which I experience firsthand inside of some kind of augmented reality headset.

I have noticed this multiway “telepathy” since 13_FEBRUARY_2019 and have attempted to discuss this with people multiple times over the years. So far, no one has seriously been able to relate to what I describe and I have not found much information on the subject while searching the web. Because I seem to have no one in my life who is knowledgeable about this “telepathy”, I feel that I inhabit a reality which is different from everyone else’s. I feel violated in a way which makes traditional rape seem trivial.

I do not have any friends at this time mostly because I have so little in common with other people and because I am in perpetual distress over how I am being treated. I suspect that I will feel obliged to get a job some time later this year (which means I will have to be well practiced at pretending to be normal and to not have these “paranormal” experiences which no one else seems to be aware of nor seriously interested in). I resent having to be so stifled, dumbed down, and cheerlessly cheerful as a skillful sycophant and crowd pleasing comedian, but that seems to be the only way to get out of unemployment as a convicted felon with no friends and a lack of credentials to get a better job than pretending to be half my age working at some movie theater concession stand.

(Please note that these writings are a bit antagonistic sounding and prejudiced and not exactly as objective as they otherwise could be. The author is venting some of its frustrations by being a bit ad hominem).

It seems that the challenge of my life is to let go of being so outwardly ambitious, arrogant, and narcissistic while I feel that there is seriously nothing wrong with me and I genuinely like who I am (and I like that I am unabashedly self centered, arrogant, and obsessed about what I care about). It seems that I live at a time where most of the people around me are a lot more subdued and complacent than I am and a lot more content to make interpersonal relationships the center of their lives than I am. It seems like all the introverts are dead and only extroverts are allowed to exist.

Perhaps introverts exist but they do not have a public web presence. Perhaps they exist but pretend to be extroverts. I think that I may actually be an extrovert who thinks it is still an introvert. The police have hollowed me out to the point that I feel that they know more about me than I do and that I have no more secrets left to hide because all of my brain activity is being decoded. As an extrovert, I am 100% public. I have no “inner life” because whatever is “inner” to me is already “outer” to someone who has the technological prowess and legal authority to publicize whatever I consider to be my “inner” experience. If I did not bother keeping this diary and making it publicly available on the Internet, I risk allowing other people whom I may or may not know to cherry pick my “inner” experience and to promote a profile about me which is tailored to suit their agenda rather than mine. Hence, I have to be my own representative so that whoever is competing with me to be my representative is not the only one speaking up about the “inner” experience of Karlina Ray Beringer.

The air is getting cold and chilly. My skin is grimy and sticky. The air by the freeway is a bit thick with pollution which I can taste and feel. After I publish this note, I am going to get on the BART train and head back to Castro Valley to take a shower at my dad’s house. Then I’m going back to that neighborhood church where I have been camping out at recently. It’s a relatively quiet, peaceful, and safe place (and it has electrical outlets and overhangs to escape the rain)!
